    Welcome.

    2124: Preserved for many years within a cryonics pod, you finally wake up unfrozen and somehow still alive. No memories, all but a little piece of paper crumbled; saying “_earth, is hopelessly lost_”

    Ecliptic Space is a puzzle-rpg mixture. This “_rpg_” won't have any physical combat, but all mental combat. You are alone and all have been lost around you and the places yet to be discovered. Overcome puzzles to gain victory. Find your identity and discover your destiny.
